HISTORY OF ISRO

INDIAN SPACE RESEARCH ORGANISATION (ISRO) is the space agency of the Government of India headquartered in the city of Bengaluru. The Indian National Committee for Space Research (INCOSPAR) was established in the tenure of Jawaharlal Nehru. under the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) in 1962 ISRO was formed (dissolved)in 15 august 1969 with a vision to develop and harness space technology in national development, while pursuing planitary exploration and space science research.... Jawaharlal Nehru and scientist VIkram sarabhai,considered amongst the founding fathers of Indaian space program. Its vision is to "harness space technology for national development while pursuing space science research & planetary exploration". In the begining Dr.Vikram sarabhai called Indian National Committee For Space Research(INCOSPAR) ,but later its called as INDIAN SPACE RESEARCH ORGANISATION(ISRO).
